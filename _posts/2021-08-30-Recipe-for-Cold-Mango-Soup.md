# Cold Mango Soup

## Created by Inna Kozachkov
- 1 Can Palm Hearts
- 1 Red Pepper
- 1 Mini (Persian) Cucumber
- 1 Tomato
- 1 Avacado
- 1/2 Onion 
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 1 Mango
- 1/2 Bottle of Kefir 
- 1/2 Tea Spoon Cayenne Pepper 

Refrigerate
